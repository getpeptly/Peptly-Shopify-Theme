# Peptly — Brand Positioning Document

**Version 1.0 | April 2026**

---

## Category

Peptly is a direct-to-consumer telehealth brand for physician-supervised peptide therapy. We connect patients with licensed providers who evaluate, prescribe, and monitor compounded peptide protocols delivered to their door.

## Target Customer

**Primary:** Men 28–55 who actively manage their health, recovery, and longevity. They lift weights, track sleep, listen to Huberman, follow biohackers on X, and have already heard of BPC-157. They have disposable income, a chronic injury or recovery goal, and a deep distrust of both Big Pharma and gray market research vendors.

**Secondary:** Masters athletes (35–55) managing chronic injuries from years of training, and longevity-focused professionals optimizing for healthspan rather than performance.

**What they want:** A trustworthy, legitimate way to access the peptides they've been reading about for years — without injecting unregulated research chemicals from anonymous overseas vendors or paying $1,000/month at a concierge anti-aging clinic.

## The Problem We Solve

Peptide therapy has been clinically interesting for decades, but until 2026 it was effectively gatekept. The two existing options both failed customers:

- **Gray market research vendors** sell peptides labeled "for research only" with no physician oversight, no compounding standards, no purity verification, and no accountability. Customers inject these compounds at their own risk.
- **Concierge anti-aging clinics** charge $500–$1,500/month for the same peptides, bundled with consultations, lab panels, and overhead that prices out everyone except the wealthy.

Peptly is the first brand to make peptide therapy legitimate, accessible, and affordable in one product.

## Positioning Statement

**For health-conscious adults who want safe access to peptide therapy, Peptly is the physician-supervised telehealth brand that delivers compounded peptide protocols at a fraction of the cost of concierge clinics — because legitimate care shouldn't require choosing between safety and affordability.**

We win the customer who wants the safety of a clinic without the price tag, and the price of a research vendor without the risk.

## Brand Promise

Every Peptly customer gets three things, every time:

1. **A licensed physician reviews your case** before any prescription is written.
2. **Your peptides come from a verified compounding pharmacy** with documented purity standards — and we publish who that pharmacy is.
3. **You pay a fair price** with no hidden consultation fees, no surprise lab charges, and no $200 "membership."

## Key Messages

- *Peptide therapy, finally done right.*
- *Real doctors. Real pharmacies. Real prices.*
- *The peptides you've been researching, prescribed by people who actually know what they're doing.*
- *No gray market. No concierge markup. Just legitimate care.*

## Brand Voice

**Confident, not arrogant.** We know the science. We don't oversell.

**Educational, not condescending.** Our customers have done their research. We respect their intelligence and add to it instead of starting from zero.

**Transparent, not corporate.** We name our medical director. We name our pharmacy partner. We publish our purity certificates. We answer hard questions directly.

**Calm, not hype-driven.** No "shocking transformation" headlines. No fake urgency. No before/after deepfakes. The product speaks for itself.

## What We Are Not

- We are not a weight loss brand. We do not sell GLP-1s.
- We are not a wellness lifestyle brand. We do not sell supplements, vitamins, or non-prescription products.
- We are not a clinic. We are a telehealth platform connecting patients with independent licensed providers.
- We are not for everyone. We are for people who want results from real medicine, not aspirational marketing.
